# Pilot-1 Company FAQ

## Returns & Refunds
We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number.

## Shipping Information
We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days domestically and 7-14 days internationally. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

## Order Tracking
You can track your order using the tracking number sent to your email after shipment. Visit our tracking page at pilot1company.com/track and enter your order number.

## Customer Support
You can reach our support team via:
- Email: support@pilot1company.com
- Phone: 1-800-PILOT1 (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- Live chat on our website (available 24/7)

## Payment Methods
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

## Order Modifications
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact customer support for assistance. We'll do our best to accommodate changes before shipment.
