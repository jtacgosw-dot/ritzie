# Pilot-1 Product Guide

## Product Categories
Our product line includes:
- Premium Electronics: Smartphones, tablets, laptops, and accessories
- Home & Garden: Furniture, decor, and outdoor equipment
- Fashion & Apparel: Clothing, shoes, and accessories for all ages
- Health & Beauty: Skincare, wellness products, and personal care

## Product Warranties
All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase. Home & garden items have a 6-month warranty, while fashion items have a 90-day quality guarantee.

## Size Guides
Detailed size charts are available on each product page. For clothing, we recommend checking measurements against our size guide. If you're between sizes, we suggest ordering the larger size for comfort.

## Product Care Instructions
Each product includes specific care instructions. Electronics should be kept in dry environments and cleaned with appropriate materials. Clothing care labels provide washing and drying instructions.

## Availability & Stock
Product availability is updated in real-time on our website. Out-of-stock items can be backordered with estimated restock dates provided. We offer email notifications when items become available.
